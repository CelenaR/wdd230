Design Principles

White Space and Clean Design

Birch Community

Birchcommunity.com

A beautiful country side nestled in London, England lays this peaceful get away from the hustle and bustle of London city life. The home page is a white, clean minimal design. The logo and navigation is easy to find. Only a few simple pictures are on the main page.

PARC : Alignment

SixnFive

Sixnfive.com

This website has unique one of a kind art pieces to admire and purchase. It also shows films from up and coming artists. The home screen is all laid out in box format. Everything is lined up from the left to the right hand side. Even the images in the center of the screen line up with each other. There is a mix of centered, vertical, and horizontal alignment.

Visual Hierarchy

Quotery

Quotery.com

This website shows exactly where it wants its viewers to look at first. The one main image for the whole background shows a bunch of books. Right in the middle of the page it wants you to use its search menu to look up a quote. There are only a few things on the main page to look at. It doesn't want its reader to get confused on what to search for on this site.
